Instructions

Sponge

  1. Preheat the oven to 140°C fan forced / 150°C convection.
  2. Line the bottom of an 8-inch cake tin with parchment paper.
  3. In a medium-sized bowl whisk the egg yolks, milk, and oil.
  4. Sift the flour into the egg yolk mixture and mix until combined.
  5. In another bowl, beat egg whites with sugar until stiff peaks form.
  6. Add ⅓ of the meringue to the yolk mixture and fold gently.
  7. Fold remaining meringue until just combined.
  8. Transfer batter to cake tin.
  9. Place in water bath and bake for 70 mins.
  10. Remove and allow to cool completely.
  11. Run knife around edges and invert.
  12. Wrap in cling film and refrigerate until assembly.

Whipped Cream

  1. Whip cream with sugar until soft peaks form.
  2. Beat until stiff peaks.

Assembly

  1. Combine sugar and water, microwave until dissolved.
  2. Slice half the strawberries.
  3. Slice cooled cake into three layers.
  4. Brush first layer with syrup.
  5. Layer with cream, strawberries, more cream. Repeat.
  6. Top with sponge and crumb coat with cream.
  7. Pipe remaining cream with a star tip.
  8. Decorate with remaining strawberries.
