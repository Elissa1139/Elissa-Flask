STICKY DATE PUDDING (AKA STICKY TOFFEE PUDDING)

A comforting classic dessert made with sweet dates and brown sugar, drizzled with warm butterscotch sauce. Top it with a scoop of ice cream for the ultimate dessert.

INGREDIENTS

DATE PUDDING

  • 140 g Pitted dates, roughly chopped (5oz)
  • 1/4 tsp Baking Soda
  • 120 g Water (1/2 cup)
  • 45 g Unsalted butter (3 tbsp)
  • 1 tbsp Molasses
  • 45 g Dark brown sugar (1/4 cup)
  • 1 Egg
  • 80 g Plain flour (2/3 cup)
  • 1/2 tsp Baking powder

BUTTERSCOTCH

  • 120 g Dark brown sugar (1/2 cup)
  • 120 g Thickened/Heavy cream (1/2 cup)
  • 95 g Unsalted butter (1/3 cup 1 tbsp)
  • 1 tbsp Molasses
  • Pinch Salt
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract

TO SERVE

  • Vanilla ice cream, optional
  • Whipped cream, optional

INSTRUCTIONS

PUDDING

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C / 355°F
  2. Grease 6 holes of a cupcake tin with butter and dust with flour
  3. Combine the chopped dates, boiling water and baking soda, let sit for 10 minutes
  4. Cream the butter, sugar and molasses together in a stand mixer or with an electric whisk
  5. Add the egg and continue beating until completely combined
  6. Then add the flour and baking powder, mix until just combined
  7. With a stick blender, or regular blender process the date and water mixture until a smooth puree
  8. Add the puree to the batter and stir until just combined
  9. Divide the batter evenly between the greased cupcake holes and bake for 15–20 minutes, or until an inserted toothpick comes out clean
  10. Whilst the puddings are baking make the butterscotch sauce

BUTTERSCOTCH SAUCE

  1. In a medium-sized saucepan melt the brown sugar, butter, and molasses together on very low heat
  2. Once all melted add the cream and vanilla, stir
